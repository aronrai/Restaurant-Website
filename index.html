About Us

At Anila's Restaurant, we take pride in serving authentic Nepali-style Thali, a wholesome and flavorful meal experience. Our Thali includes a variety of carefully prepared dishes to satisfy your taste buds.

Veg Thali

Our Veg Thali features:

Steamed white rice

Comforting lentil soup (dal)

Seasonal vegetable curry

Freshly prepared pickles and accompaniments

Non-Veg Thali

For non-vegetarian lovers, our Non-Veg Thali offers all the essentials of the Veg Thali, along with a choice of succulent meat options, including:

Chicken

Fish

Buff (buffalo meat)

Pork

Each Thali is crafted to bring you the essence of Nepali home-cooked meals. Visit Anila's Restaurant and let us take you on a delicious journey to Nepal!
